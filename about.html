About Me

I’m Michelle Stephan, a Licensed Clinical Mental Health Counselor, passionate about helping individuals find hope and healing. I earned my Master of Arts in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from the University of Mary Hardin-Baylor in Belton, Texas.

My journey into the mental health field began in 2016 during my practicum and internship experiences at various centers. I’ve worked in residential treatment and group practice settings in Asheville, NC, eventually leading to the birth of Auden Counseling in 2018.

I understand the challenges life can present and believe that no one should have to navigate them alone. I offer support, empathy, and guidance using evidence-based approaches tailored to individual needs.
